Key Features of Our App

Inventory Management

Real-time inventory visibility benefits businesses in the pharmaceutical industry. Local pharmacies can manage stock levels, reduce shortages, and avoid overstocking. Stockists efficiently supply pharmacies, ensuring timely replenishment. Pharmaceutical companies gain insights for production planning, minimizing shortages and overproduction.

Medicine Ordering

Providing a centralized platform for pharmacies and stockists to place orders. It eliminates the need for manual phone calls or paper-based notes, streamlining the entire ordering process. This feature enhances efficiency, reduces errors, and speeds up order fulfillment.

Demand Forecasting

The app uses advanced demand forecasting to predict future demand in the pharmaceutical industry. This helps companies optimize production, inventory, and distribution. By doing so, they can avoid shortages and overproduction, saving costs and improving efficiency.

Role Based

Pharmanuman is a role-based application that caters to different users in the pharmaceutical industry. Local pharmacies can manage inventory, place orders. Stockists have features for efficient supply and inventory management. Pharmaceutical companies gain insights for production planning, inventory optimization, and demand forecasting.
